# Privacy Policy – FocusTab

FocusTab does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All data (such as focus text and streaks) is stored locally on the user's device using browser storage.

We do not use external servers, analytics, or tracking tools.

This extension is designed purely for productivity and user experience.
